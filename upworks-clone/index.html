Supplier Engagement

We work with suppliers that meet the needs of our business and align with our mission. We evaluate the environmental, social, and governance practices of our supply base and seek to work with diverse suppliers whenever possible.

Our Areas of Impact

Managing a responsible supply chain

We're always looking to partner with suppliers that support our mission and demonstrate their own commitments to sustainable business practices. We assess the social and environmental performance of our suppliers and those interested in partnership.

Read our Supplier Code of Conduct

Supporting inclusive sourcing

Upwork's Inclusive Sourcing program is designed to help underrepresented communities compete, build wealth, and thrive. We're building a diverse portfolio of suppliers for Upwork's procurement needs and connecting diverse businesses on our work marketplace with clients looking for diverse talent.
